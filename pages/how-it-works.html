How IWLI Works

A seamless end-to-end workflow from sensor data collection to compliance reporting.

01

IoT Sensors Collect Data

Smart sensors on industrial bins continuously monitor fill levels, weight, temperature, humidity, and contamination. Data is streamed to the IWLI platform in near real-time (configurable, e.g. every 30–60 seconds).

02

AI Classifies & Analyzes

The OpenAI-powered engine classifies waste types (Hazardous, Industrial, Organic), detects contamination, calculates purity scores, and generates actionable recommendations for your operations team.

03

Alerts Are Generated Automatically

When thresholds are crossed — overfill, contamination, temperature or gas anomalies — the system generates severity-graded alerts (Low, Medium, High, Critical) and notifies the right personnel instantly.

04

Routes Are Optimized

Pickup routes for waste collection trucks are automatically optimized using advanced algorithms. The system tracks live fleet positions, calculates CO₂ savings, and logs distance and fuel efficiency per route.

05

Reports Are Generated

Compliance documents for CPCB/SPCB regulators, ESG impact reports, and ROI summaries are generated automatically — ready to export as CSV or PDF at any time.
