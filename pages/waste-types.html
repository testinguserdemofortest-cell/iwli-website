Industrial Waste Classification

IWLI's AI engine identifies and manages all 11+ industrial waste types across three major categories.

⚠ Hazardous

Chemical Waste

Solvents, acids, bases, and reactive compounds requiring special handling and disposal protocols.

⚠ Hazardous

Electronic Waste

Circuit boards, batteries, and electronic components with heavy metals and toxic materials.

⚠ Hazardous

Radioactive Waste

Low and medium-level radioactive materials from industrial processes, tracked with strict compliance.

◈ Industrial

Scrap Metal

Ferrous and non-ferrous metals with real-time market rate tracking and buyer matching.

◈ Industrial

Packaging Waste

Plastics, cardboard, and composite packaging materials classified for recycling or disposal.

◈ Industrial

Construction Debris

Concrete, brick, tiles, and demolition materials handled with compliant disposal routing.

◈ Industrial

Textile & Wood

Industrial offcuts and fabric waste from manufacturing tracked for recycling and energy recovery.

✦ Organic

Food & Organic

Biodegradable waste from processing facilities, routed for composting or biogas conversion.

✦ Organic

Recyclable Materials

Mixed recyclables sorted and tracked by material composition and market value.

✦ Organic

Composite Waste

Multi-material items requiring pre-processing before recycling or certified disposal.
