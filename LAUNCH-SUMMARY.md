## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK LINKS

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Domain not found | Wait 24h for DNS, check at mxtoolbox.com |
| SSL not working | Clear cache, wait 24h, check ssllabs.com |
